Salem

The Story

In December of 2018 I accepted a role at Salem Church of God as their resident of Outreach an Communications. I was tasked with coordinating outreach and mission efforts as well as designing Salem's new Web and app Interface

Constraints

  • Time
  • Money
  • Ease of Use

Objective

Utilize a web management software to design a quick and fast webpage and mobile app to be used to leverage social resources to promote growth and volunteerism. Salem required a web and mobile platform that would be easy for someone who is not extensively trained to be able to view, understand, and make changes

Project Solution

Weighing all of our goals and constraints, I decided to use Subsplash, a church based web building platform that was cheap and would be fairly easy for me to train staff with no prior experience.

Design Process

I broke each aspect of the web and mobile design into easy to chunks focusing on each ministry area separately. One each chunk was completed I allowed members of our staff to test and provide feed back. Once a draft of the full site and mobile app was finished I opened use up to the entire congregation along with a survey to give us feedback on problems. Over the next few weeks I released several versions of the app and website taking into account all the feedback.

Results

Since it's deployment our app and website has seen over 1800 downloads. Users have been able to utilize the app to interact with the church follow along with messages, listen to podcast, communicate with each other, sign up to volunteer, schedule visits and so much more!
